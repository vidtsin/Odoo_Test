Gate Entry

Prixgen Tech Solutions introduces Gate Entry functionality where, whenever a vehicle / consignment enters or leaves the primises of a plant, Inward / Outward Gate Entry is done capturing the details of the goods and the vehicle..

Inward Gate Entry

Inward Gate Entry form captures, from where the vehicle is, products in the vehicle, LR/RR No., Vehicle No., Odometer Value to keep track of the kilometers travelled by the vehicle.

Source No. field in the line part filters the document based on the tab selected, whether it is Purchases , Sales Returns or Internal Transfers

Outward Gate Entry

Outward Gate Entry form captures, from where the vehicle is, products in the vehicle, LR/RR No., Vehicle No., Odometer Value to keep track of the kilometers travelled by the vehicle

Source No. field in the line part filters the document based on the tab selected, whether it is Sales , Purchase Returns or Internal Transfers

Inward & Outward widget at Sales and Purchase Module

Congifuration for Warehouse Users

Option provided at Gate Entry configuration to map the respective user with respective warehouse to indicate whether Gate Entry is required for the selected warehouse. Based on this configuration, Gate Entry is validated at the shipment level.

New field at Shipment Level

Gate Entry done is validated at the time of shipment, where a option is provided at shipment level to attach the respective Gate Entry for the respective shipment document.
